Learn with a master

People worldwide have begun to explore more healthy cooking styles particularly ones that employ vegetables and fish as primary ingredients. For this reason, Japanese cuisine enjoys a growing popularity on its own and is part of a general movement toward “fusion” cooking styles.

These classes will provide each student a more advanced knowledge and practical application of Japanese cuisine and its preparation.

Chef Matsuda is an experienced teacher of Japanese culinary arts and a food consultant representing Japanese foods and cuisine in the United States, Europe and Asia with more than 30 years of experience.

Onigiri

Onigiri, translated as “rice ball”, is white rice shaped into a triangle or ball with a type of filling that is wrapped in seaweed. Learn how to make this classic Japanese staple with many delicious fillings including salted salmon,pickled plums, and tuna!

$25

Bento

A traditional bento holds rice or noodles, fish or meat, with pickled and cooked vegetables, in a box. In this class you will how to prepare a beautiful Japanese style lunch box with meat, vegetables, and rice.

$40

Ramen

Learn how to make ramen like the masters in Tokyo! In this class you will learn how to make a chicken-based ramen with seasoned hard-boiled egs, and mouth-watering pork slices.

$45

Sushi

Japan’s most famous cuisine, sushi, is a special Japanese dish consisting of a carefully cut piece fish laid on top of vinegar rice. In this class you will learn how to prepare a decadent 10-piece sushi course.

$65
